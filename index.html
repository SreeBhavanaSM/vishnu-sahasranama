श्री विष्णु सहस्रनामम्

अन्तर्मुख समाराध्या
बहिर्मुख सुदुर्लभा

Antarmukha samārādhya, bahirmukha sudurlabha

The Divine is easily worshipped by those who turn inward — but is very difficult to find by those who seek only outward.

Sandhi & Meaning

antarmukha = antara (inner) + mukha (direction)

samārādhya = sam + ā + rādh (to worship completely)

bahirmukha = bahira (outer) + mukha (direction)

sudurlabha = su + dur + labha (very difficult to attain)
